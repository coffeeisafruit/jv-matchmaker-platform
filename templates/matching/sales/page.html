How It Works

Three steps. No learning curve.

1

We build your partner profile

You complete a short intake — your offer, your audience, your niche, and what you're looking for in a partner. This profile drives every match decision, every month.

2

Our system finds your matches

We run your profile against a database of over one million vetted business profiles using a proprietary matching system. It evaluates each potential partner for audience alignment, partnership intent, mutual value, and business readiness — and surfaces the ones most likely to be a genuine fit.

3

You get your report — monthly

Your report arrives with everything you need to reach out. Review your matches, read why each one fits, copy the outreach template, and start your conversations.

Behind the scenes: our database draws from 35+ sources including dedicated JV platforms, podcast directories, coaching networks, and speaker bureaus. Every profile is researched and enriched with AI — we know what each person does, who they serve, and what they're looking for. Match quality is reviewed by a human expert panel before any match type reaches your report.
