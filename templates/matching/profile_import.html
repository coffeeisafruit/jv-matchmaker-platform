Import Profiles from CSV

Bulk import JV partner profiles from a CSV file.

CSV Format Requirements

Your CSV file should include a header row with the following columns:

  • name (required) - Full name of the contact
  • company - Company name
  • email - Email address
  • linkedin_url - LinkedIn profile URL
  • website_url - Website URL
  • industry - Industry category
  • audience_size - One of: tiny, small, medium, large, massive
  • audience_description - Description of their audience
  • content_style - Their content style and topics

Sample CSV Format

name,company,email,linkedin_url,website_url,industry,audience_size,audience_description,content_style
John Smith,Acme Corp,john@acme.com,https://linkedin.com/in/johnsmith,https://acme.com,Technology,medium,Tech entrepreneurs and developers,Educational long-form content
Jane Doe,Marketing Pro,jane@marketingpro.com,https://linkedin.com/in/janedoe,https://marketingpro.com,Marketing,large,Small business owners,Practical tips and case studies
