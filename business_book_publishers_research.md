# KEY SUBMISSION TIPS FOR 2026

1. **Business book proposals** sell books - complete manuscripts rarely needed upfront
2. **Platform matters** - publishers want authors with speaking, consulting, or media presence
3. **Specificity wins** - "Leading Remote Asynchronous Engineering Teams" beats "Leadership"
4. **Word count:** 45,000-60,000 words ideal for trade business books
5. **Response times:** 4-12 weeks typical, up to 6 months for some
6. **Include:** Market analysis, competitive titles, marketing plan, author platform details
7. **Niche targeting:** "CMOs at mid-sized tech firms struggling with AI adoption" beats "business owners"
