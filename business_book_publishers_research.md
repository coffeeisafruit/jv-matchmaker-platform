## SUBMISSION TIPS

1. **Business book proposals** are preferred over complete manuscripts
2. **Platform matters** - publishers want authors with speaking, consulting, or media presence
3. **Specificity wins** - "Leading Remote Asynchronous Engineering Teams" beats "Leadership"
4. **Response times:** 4-12 weeks typical, up to 6 months for some
5. **Include:** Market analysis, competitive titles, marketing plan, author platform details
